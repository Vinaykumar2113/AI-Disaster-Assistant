# AI Disaster Response Assistant - Project Plan
The AI Disaster Response Assistant is a full-stack AI-powered web application designed to assist citizens and emergency response teams during natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, cyclones, landslides, and fires.

The system enables citizens to report disasters, receive AI-generated safety guidance, locate nearby emergency shelters, and monitor the status of their emergency requests. Emergency officers can manage incidents, coordinate rescue operations, and communicate with affected citizens through a centralized platform.

## Problem Statement

During disasters, people often struggle to obtain accurate emergency information, locate nearby shelters, and contact emergency responders quickly.

Most existing solutions are fragmented across different platforms.

This project aims to provide one centralized AI-powered emergency response platform.
## Objectives
* Build a modern disaster response platform.
* Integrate Artificial Intelligence for emergency guidance.
* Enable real-time disaster reporting.
* Help citizens locate nearby shelters.
* Assist emergency officers in rescue coordination.
* Provide a scalable software architecture.
